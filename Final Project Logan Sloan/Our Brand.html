Coke Locker

Beverage Distribution Company

Coke Locker is a Beverage Distribution Company based out of Heritage Christian School and was founded in 2020. It's a customer streamlined service that offers ice cold beverages on demand for the best price. Customer's can purchase cans from Locker 508 or from a Cooled Case carried by a Coke Locker associate.

Humble Beginnings

The History of Coke Locker

Coke Locker found it's beginnings with one young man, a cooler, a locker, and a dream. The Coke Locker first debuted on March 4 2020 with Logan Sloan carrying and selling a modest 6 cans. it wasn't much but it was a promising start. From there, he began building inventory, customers, and a brand. In the first week of business, 53 cans were sold and the Coke Locker was born.
